Transport

Knight Bus

The Knight Bus is summoned by raising your wand arm. It is fitted inside with four-poster beds, and you can get a toothbrush or a mug of hot chocolate if you choose; though it's probably best to skip the latter, as the ride is rough enough to cause you to spill your mug. The Knight Bus appears in its daytime configuration. The bus could travel anywhere on land but did not travel underwater. The four-poster beds are replaced by ordinary chairs, which like the beds are not anchored, so they slide around the inside of the bus with every swerve. Although it is also possible to book tickets for travel on the bus in advance.

Platform 9 3/4

Platform Nine and Three Quarters is reached by leaning against, or running through, the barrier between platforms 9 and 10 at King's Cross Station. It is apparently physically located within the confines of the station, though perhaps at a lower level, as the train does seem to pass through an appropriate area of London as it leaves the city. Leaving the platform is generally done by walking through an archway, and a wizard railway guard allows only a few students through at a time to prevent alarming the Muggles when a large number of students appear through a solid brick wall.

Flying car

The 1960 Ford Angila 105E featured in Harry potter and the chamber of secretes. Enhanced by Ron Weasley,s father Arthur, the car could fly, become invisible, and never run out of fuel.
