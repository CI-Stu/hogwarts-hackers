Transport

Knight Bus

The Knight Bus is summoned by raising your wand arm. During the day time the bus is fitted with seats, however night-time travellers can enjoy the luxurious comfort of a four-poster bed. Tooth brushes and hot chocolate are available, though it's probably best to skip the latter, as the ride is rough enough to cause you to spill your mug.

Travel time from all locations is estimated to take between 20 and 40 muggle seconds.

Advanced bookings are not available. Prices start at 11 Sickles for a single fare (hot choclate and tooth brush not included) and 1 Galleon for a return.

Platform 9 3/4

Platform Nine and Three Quarters is reached by leaning against, or running through, the barrier between platforms 9 and 10 at King's Cross Station.

The journey from Kings Cross to Hogsmeade take 7 hours through glorious countryside, so you can sit back, relax and enjoy the picturesque scenery. Refreshments avaiable, including: Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans, Cauldron Cakes, Chocolate Frogs, Drooble's Best Blowing Gum, Jelly Slugs, Liquorice wands, Pumpkin Pasties and much more.

Advanced bookings are not available. Prices start at 11 Sickles for a single fare and 1 Galleon for a return.

Flying Car

If travelling by flying car then please set wizardnav to:
'Weasley NCP Car Park, 186 Crookshanks Lane, Hogsmeade, HG89 4HP'. Parking is charged at 3 Galleon Per Day.

Directions:
Hogsmeade is located just off cloud 8, juntion 2. Use the sky ring road and follow sign posting to the main car park.

Broomstick parking available for 1 Galleon per day
